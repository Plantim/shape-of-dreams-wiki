Worlds

Each run in the game consist of 4 world, after finishing 4-th world, traveler can choose to either face the Dream Guardian, or go deeper into the dream and fight through 4 more worlds. These choise repeats untill traveler lose all HP or slay Dream Guardian.
Sometime you can find an item in Souveneir shop to force next world to be one of specific choice, other then that, they picked at random.

World 1

First world is always Forest of Turbulence with it's Guardian - Belphomet (Demon of the Forest).
Forest looking locations with spiders, trees, charging tree creatures enemies.

World 2

We have a random pick from 3 options, they generally have 1/3 chance to appear:

1) Land of Pyrana with Guardian - Infernus (Burning Giant).
Lava/Mountains looking locations with fire creatures enemies.

2) Forgotten Cave of Naraka with Guardian - Seeker.
Cave looking locations with shadow like creatures enemies.

3) Shattered Snowy Mountain with Guardian - Skoll (Snowy Mountain King).
Snowy Mountain looking locations with ice theme creatures enemies.

World 3

We have a random pick from 2 options, statisticly seems the first option have 2/3 chance to appear and second is twice time rarer:

1) Fragment of the Starry Sky with Guardians - Nyx /or/ Erebos.
Sky themed locations with orb/ghost looking creature enemies.
Second boss can be summoned if you finish the "Raven" quest, but don't use his portal.

2) Whispering Lotus Plateau with Guardians - Dark Moon /or/ White Night.
Japaneese themed locations with samurai/swords creatures enemies.
Second or even both Guardians of Impermanence can be summoned by praying at the altars through the locations. 3 pray will summon White Night, 5-6 will summon both.

World 4

Last world is always Eroded Isle of Despair with it's Guardian - Azraq.
Cave/Island looking locations connected by transportation plant. Rats, wasps and some crab creatures enemies.

Lucid Dreams

Good

Bon Voyage - Hunters don't chase the expedition.
False Lifeline - Upon completing a location, all Travelers recover 25% of their lost health, and fallen Travelers are revived.

Evil

Fish Scales - The amount of shield you can be maintain at once is capped at 100% of your maximum health, and monster attacks deal damage to the Traveler's maximum health. You can mutigate some of this max health damage by activating a Shrine of Guidance, and the damage is reset each time you enter a new world.
Grevious Wounds - All healing and Barriers received by Travelers are reduced by 50%.
Mad Life - All monsters try harder to predict the Travelers' movements. (They may not always succed.)
Marsh of Destiny - Each time you enter a new world, choose a random torment.
Overpopulation - Increases the number of monsters by 50%, excluding bosses.
Prudent Jellyfish - Most cooldown reduction effects are weakened.

Chaos

Embrace Mortality - All characters deal 100% increased damage.
Harmless Whispers - Most Heroic Boses can now be affected by crowd control effects, but their maximum health and damage are slightly increased.
Sparkling Dream Flask - Health Potions and Shrine of Guidance now grant Dream Dust instead of healing.
The Darkest Urge - All characters are unable to distinguish between friend and foe, and monsters devour each other to grow stronger.
WILD - All monsters except bosses are summoned as Hunters. Hunters start moving immediately when you enter a world, but the Hunt Level resets each time you move to the next world.
